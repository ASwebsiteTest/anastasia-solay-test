About Anastasia

Born in San Francisco and based between Geneva and the international music scene, singer-songwriter Anastasia Solay creates emotionally driven music that blends personal storytelling with modern electronic and pop influences.

Her songs explore the human experience—love, connection, resilience, personal growth, and the world around us. Each track reflects her journey as an artist and her ability to transform life experiences into honest, memorable melodies.

Drawing inspiration from a wide range of musical influences, from classical composers such as Tchaikovsky to contemporary icons like Tupac, Anastasia brings together diverse sounds and perspectives to create a unique artistic identity.

As the songwriter, composer, producer, and creative director of her projects, Anastasia maintains a strong artistic vision from the first idea to the final production. Her collaborations add new dimensions to her music while preserving the authenticity and emotional depth at the center of every song.

Her latest project, Sonic Boom, explores themes of love, hope, and the value of every moment, inviting listeners to connect through shared emotions and experiences.
